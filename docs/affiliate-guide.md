# TradersUtopia Affiliate Portal — Getting Started

Welcome to the new affiliate portal. Your existing commissions, sales history, and earnings have all been moved over — nothing is lost. This guide walks you through everything you need to know.

---

## 1. Signing In

Go to **tradersutopia-affilate-portal.vercel.app** and sign in with:

- **Google** (recommended — use the same email as your affiliate account), or
- **Email + password**

On your first sign-in, the portal automatically links your existing account and loads your full commission history. You'll see a banner showing the progress — this usually takes under a minute.

> **Tip:** If the banner gets stuck for more than a couple of minutes, sign out and sign back in. That kicks it again.

---

## 2. The Dashboard

Your home screen shows:

- **Total earned** — lifetime payout, shown in CAD by default
- **This month earned** — commissions from the current month
- **Commission count** — every sale you've been credited for
- **Attendance days this month** — more on this below
- **Recent commissions** — your 5 most recent sales

A **currency toggle (CAD / USD)** sits in the top right. Exchange rates are live.

---

## 3. Your Commissions

Click **Commissions** in the sidebar to see your full history. Each row shows:

- The sale date
- Your cut
- A status badge:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Earned** | Counted, waiting to be paid |
| **Paid** | Money has been sent to you |
| **Forfeited** | Did not count (see Attendance below) |
| **Pending** | Rate setup is still in progress — will convert to Earned once your rate is confirmed |
| **Voided** | Sale was refunded or charged back |

You can filter by status and date range.

---

## 4. Attendance — The Most Important New Thing

This is the biggest change from before. **Commissions are now tied to whether you're actively marketing.**

### How it works

- On days you do marketing work, go to **Attendance** and submit an attendance record.
- If a sale comes in on a day you **didn't** submit attendance, your cut is forfeited. (The sale still happens — you just don't earn from it.)
- There's a **±1 day window** — the portal checks the day before, day of, and day after the sale to account for time zones.

### What about your past commissions?

You're safe. There's a grace rule: the portal does **not** forfeit any commission that came in *before* you submitted your first-ever attendance. Your entire pre-portal history is counted normally.

Once you submit your first attendance, the rule activates from that day forward. So only start submitting when you're actually doing marketing work.

### Forgot a day?

If a sale was marked forfeited and you realize you actually did market that day, submit attendance for that date. The portal will automatically recover any forfeited commissions that now fall inside the attendance window.

## 5. Promo Codes

Click **Promo Codes** to request a new code.

- Codes must be **4–6 letters**, letters only
- Your request goes to your teacher (if you have one) or an admin for approval
- Once approved, the code is automatically activated — no further action needed
- You'll get a notification when it's approved or rejected

---

## 6. Teacher / Student View (if applicable)

If you're someone's teacher, you'll see a **Students** tab in the sidebar.

- Each student appears as a card showing their unpaid balance (your cut), total paid, conversion count, and attendance this month
- If your student is also a teacher themselves, their students appear nested under their card — click to expand
- Click any student card to see their full commission history and attendance records

If you're *a* student of someone, nothing in your view changes. Their cut comes out of the sale math behind the scenes — the amount shown on your commission rows is already your cut only. You never see the full sale amount.

---

## 7. Notifications

The portal sends push notifications for:

- New commissions earned
- Commissions forfeited (missed attendance)
- Promo code approved or rejected
- Payment received

### Install the portal as an app

For the best notification experience, install the portal like an app:

- **iPhone:** Open in Safari → tap the **Share** icon → **Add to Home Screen**
- **Android:** Open in Chrome → tap the three-dot menu → **Install app** (or **Add to Home Screen**)
- **Desktop:** Chrome or Edge will show an install icon in the address bar

Once installed, push notifications work like any other app.

## 8. Quick FAQ

**Q: I don't see some of my old commissions.**
A: Sign out + sign back in. If they still don't appear, contact an admin.

**Q: A sale got refunded — what happens?**
A: The commission is automatically marked as **Voided** and you'll get a notification. If it had already been marked Paid, the payout is reversed.

**Q: Why was my commission marked Forfeited?**
A: You didn't submit attendance for that day (or the day before / day after). This is the attendance rule — submit attendance on marketing days going forward to prevent this.

**Q: Can I submit attendance retroactively?**
A: Yes — submit for any past date. If a forfeited commission from that date falls back into the window, it recovers automatically.

**Q: How do I know my teacher or mentor is getting their cut?**
A: They see their cut separately in their own Students view. It doesn't affect your commission amount, which is already your share only.

**Q: The portal shows a "Stale" badge on some data — what does that mean?**
A: It means the portal couldn't refresh the latest numbers from the backend right this second, so it's showing the most recent cached value. Try refreshing the page — it usually clears up within seconds.
